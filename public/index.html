Billing & Payments

Pay as you grow with our new pricing plans. With Automated Growth Protection and hourly billing for exceeding plan limitations, you can rest assured that your auctions are stable and secure while paying a fair and competitive price.

Start by selecting a plan based on the number of concurrently active bidders you expect for your auctions, and we’ll take care of the rest. You will pay your monthly plan premium at the start of each month, and then you will be billed at the end of each month in hour increments for any overage activities only for the hours you used.

With our built-in business intelligence statistics, you can easily monitor your active bidders. With this information, you can track bidder trends and activities, and you can adjust your plan as needed. You will be able to monitor any overages, so there will be no surprise charges at the end of the month.
